introduction

Karkaladevi English Boarding Schook is popularly known as KEBS School, is established in 2060 BS (2004 AD)and located at Manthali, Ramechhap. It is the socially and economically inspired and rapidly growing institution with the commitment to impart quality, competitive and good cultured education. The core objectives were to establish academic institutions, and to provide students with quality education. Even though it is a private school, it provides certain scholarships for meritorious student, student of poor socioeconomic status and disadvantaged groups. The students are drawn from different regions of the district, diverse culture and ethnicity allowing them to engage in intercultural understanding and to experience a rich and friendly learning environment. Lastly, The school provides students with ample opportunities to engage in co–curricular and extra–curricular activities that ensure their holistic development which enables them to succeed not only in education but in life as well.This school instills in students moral values such as integrity, responsibility, empathy, collaboration and respect that are essential for students to be a good human being while nurturing their full potential.
